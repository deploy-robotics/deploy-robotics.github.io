Our process

The loop that runs your floor.

01 / 04: Plug in

Connect in days,
not months.

We place a generalist robot at your packing station. No long setup cycles, no reprogramming. Operational in days.

02 / 04: Run

Robot takes

Kitting, custom packing, and returns processing. The robot runs autonomously from day one, guided by state-of-the-art generalist models.
